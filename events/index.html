The Butler Did It


Overview

Isn't it amazing to see the detective hidden within you reaching the very roots of the perfect crime and unmask the culprit as the case unfolds? This is the event where we let people don the hat of a detective as they solve cleverly crafted mysteries. Let the detective within you arise…

Rules
  • The number of participants will be 2 per team.
  • Teams will be briefed about a case in the form of a movie.
  • The movie will not be repeated again.
  • Teams will be given sufficient but limited time to investigate the case on the basis of evidences.
  • Teams will not be allowed to discuss anything with other teams.
  • The participating teams have to write an appropriate and convincing account of what might have occurred on the crime scene including the role of the characters involved, modus, operandi, motives etc.
  • The teams then have to present their story to the judges, who may question the participants about the various aspects of their views on the case.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and abiding.
  • Violation of any rule will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.

Jobless


Overview

JOBLESS is one of the popular flagship events of our cultural Fest BLITHCHRON. Jobless is a unique and challenging event wherein, the participants get a taste of the atmosphere and ambience that prevails in a job interview.

Rules
  • The event consists of 3 rounds.
  • The first round is a written round wherein the participants will be given an hour to solve a general IQ test.
  • Based on the scores in round one, participants will be shortlisted for round two.
  • Round two is a group discussion.
  • The round two participants will be asked to discuss in a group on the topic allotted to them for 20 mins.
  • Based on the performance of participants in the group discussion, the judges will shortlist participants for round three.
  • In round three, each of the contestant will be handed over a fake resume at random and will be given 30 mins to go through it.
  • Thereafter, each contestant will be interviewed by the judges for an appropriate job according his/her allotted resume.
  • Based on the performance in the final round, the judges will announce a winner and a runner up for the event - JOBLESS 2016.

Skirmish


Overview

A platform to showcase speed, thought, intelligence and the brilliance of coordinated minds, a gaming arena to all those gamers out there to outperform all others in a competition to bring out the best.

Rules for Counter Strike
  • Knock-out match between two teams of 5 players each.
  • Coin toss is done. The winner gets to choose either CT or T.
  • The map that is to be played must be from any of these: de_dust2, de_inferno, de_train, de_nuke, de_aztec. The map is selected by mutual agreement of teams that are going to compete.
  • Race to 11. (First team to reach 11 round wins will win the match)
  • Teams swap sides after 10 rounds (from CT to T and vice versa).
  • Note: You cannot bring your own cfg files or no mods/cheats are allowed. If any player is found using illegal means, it will result in disqualification of the team from the tournament.
Rules for NFS
  • Four players compete against each other.
  • In elimination round, 3 races shall be played, Drag, Sprint and Circuit.
  • First two players having minimum time combining all three races will qualify for next round.
  • In final round, four players will be selected and five races will be played, 2 sprint, 2 circuit and 1 drag. Total minimum time of all five races will decide the rankings.
  • Note: No modified cars will be allowed. All cars available in the game should be allowed to use.
Rules for FIFA
  • 5 minutes of half time match , tournament will be knockout type.
  • 1 vs 1 player match.
  • There can be two players in the same team such that one can play the first half while other can play the second half.
  • Note: No mods/cheats are allowed.
Rules for Age of Empires II
  • Two teams consisting of two players each will compete with each other.
  • Any map can be selected based on mutual agreement of competing teams among the following: Arabia, Arena, Black forest, Coastal.
  • Civilizations can be of your choice. Population limit: 75
  • Note: No mods or cheats allowed.

Technical Events


Laser Assassins
A real life gaming experience of counter strike with laser guns, high tech laser sensor jackets and adventurous arena. The participants will be suited up with state of the art laser game equipment.
Rules
  • Online and on -spot registrations.
  • Two teams consisting of two players will be playing against each other at a time.
  • Kill the opponents as many as you can and also fetch the gold from a definite location.
  • Time limitation is 10 minutes.
  • If a player got hit more than 10 times on his jacket, his jacket will be deactivated and one red light will be illuminated on jacket activating the alarm, which will indicate that player is eliminated.

Robo Sumo
Experience the wrestling of robots where 4 bots challenge each other until one single bot finally survives the battle. Bots will be provided.
Rules
  • Online and on -spot registrations.
  • Four players compete for survival in a ring.
  • Other rules will be explained at the venue.

Robo Slide Hockey
Wanna challenge your opponent for the fastest goal? Robo slide hockey is a real-time slide hockey played using robotic arms. Tackle your opponent by your fastest reaction possible to win.
Rules
  • Online and on -spot registrations.
  • Two players compete each other.
  • The one who scores 10 points first will be the winner
  • Other rules are similar to slide hockey and will be explained at the venue.

Robo Kabaddi
Experience the fun of real kabaddi with robots. Two teams of 3 players each will play kabaddi with bots. Bots will be provided.
Rules
  • Online and on -spot registrations.
  • Two teams consisting of two players each compete each other.
  • Other rules are similar to slide hockey and will be explained at the venue.
  • A 10 minute Kabaddi match in which the higher scoring team wins.
  • Other rules are similar to kabaddi and will be explained at the venue.
